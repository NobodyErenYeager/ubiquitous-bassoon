Add GameDev.tv To Your Mobile Device:

Android Process

  1. Using Chrome, navigate to http://community.gamedev.tv.

  2. Click on the 3 dots in the top right corner of the window.

  3. If accessing the GameDev.tv community site, you can select "Install app"

  4. For regular websites you can click "Add to Home screen"

  5. If installed as an app, navigate to your apps, click and hold on the app icon, and drag it to your desired home screen page.

iOS Process

  1. Go to https://community.gamedev.tv in Safari.

  2. Navigate to your favourite busy place on the site.

  3.  Share icon then "Add To Home Screen"

  4. Click "Add" on "Add to Home Screen" window.
